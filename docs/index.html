Pomodoro, but more satisfying.

Work in focused sprints, take real breaks, repeat.
realpomo keeps it simple — so you can keep moving.

Focus sprints (Pomodoro)

Set a 25-minute sprint (or any length), stay heads-down, then take a short break.
It's a small structure that helps your day feel lighter and more intentional.

A dial you can feel

Set time by dragging the hand around the clock. Subtle winding sounds and a calm completion chime make starting — and finishing — feel natural.

Fits your day — not the other way around.

Quick presets

Start instantly with common session lengths. Perfect when your day keeps changing and you just need a timer, now.

Show the numbers (or don't)

Keep the digital timer visible for precision, or hide it to stay calm and follow the dial.

Themes that match your vibe

Pick a color theme that feels right for your workspace — focused, cozy, neutral, whatever helps you stay in flow.

Open source. Yours to keep.
